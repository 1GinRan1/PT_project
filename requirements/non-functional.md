# Non-Functional Requirements

* **Performance:** The game must maintain a stable 60 FPS on a mid-range PC configuration.
* **Loading Time:** Initial level loading must not exceed 10 seconds on an SSD.
* **Visual Style:** The interface and environment must adhere to the general design concept.
* **Control Responsiveness:** Input lag must not be noticeable to the player.
* **Compatibility:** The product must work correctly on Windows 10/11 operating systems.
